Polar Bears

Fun Facts about Polar Bears

Polar bears are very cool creatures. They are fascinating animals. Polar bears are found in the arctic where it is -50 degrees fahrenheit. You might ask "How can they survive in that cold of a place?", well the answer is as simple as another coat and some fat. Polar bears wear a white coat on top to make themselves very difficult to spot. They also have another coat that is black. The black coat is used to absorb heat, and reflects the light that is put on it. This gives a transparent look to anything that might be looking for the predator. Polar bears are the largest species of bears and are the largest carnivore on land. They can be about 450kg if it is a male and 225kg if it is a female. The heaviest polar ever weighed was 1,000kg!
