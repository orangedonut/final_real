What They Eat

Polar bears eat a variety of foods. They have been known to eat lots of meat, and rarely vegetation. Polar Bears feed mainly on seals. They eat different types of seals, such as the ringed seals and the bearded seals. These seals are the largest part of the polar bears diet. They have also been known to eat beluga whales,walrus', and bowhead whale carcasses. They have also been found to eat bird's egg's and fruits. Polar bears will travel great distances to get the food they need for them and their young.
